LiSEH Summer School

written by on

John McCrae and Thierry Declerck lectured on the topic of linguistic linked open data at the Spring School on Linked Data and the Semantic Web for Humanities Research. In this course, they gave information about key technologies for the use of linguistic linked data in the context of digital humanities research, in particular the OntoLex-lemon module and how to navigate the linguistic linked open data cloud.
